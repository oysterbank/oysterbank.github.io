About

I'm Kris Laratta, and I'm currently based in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States.

I've been a Software Engineer for over 4 years, doing things like contributing to enterprise software using Python and Django, writing C# & Javascript front-ends for SQL databases, and building mobile apps for iOS and Android.

I have a degree in Philosophy, with a focus primarily on Continental and Buddhist thought.

I also like to make music and poetry-- currently I'm studying jazz theory and bluegrass on guitar, and compiling a couple volumes of poetry. I can often be found biking around the city of Portland.
