About

My name is Kris Laratta, and I currently live in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States.

I've been a Software Engineer for over 4 years, doing things like contributing to enterprise software using Python and Django, writing C# & Javascript front-ends for SQL databases, and building mobile apps for both iOS and Android.

I have a degree in Philosophy, and a lingering interest in the thoughts of many 20-century philosophers, as well as in the implications of Tiantai Buddhism.

I also like to play music and write poetry. I've authored 2 or 3 short stories, and would like to write more some day. I can often be found biking around the city of Portland.
